Information Retrieval in RDF-Data Survey

This survey aims to compare 3 different search methods on two datasets of RDF-data.

You will be presented with a query and the information need/intent behind the query. Your task is to rate how relevant you pervieve each result given to be compared to the given query and intent.

Each result should be ranked with a score on a scale of 0-3 where 0 is not relevant and 3 is relevant. Each result has to be given a rating before you can submit the answer.
