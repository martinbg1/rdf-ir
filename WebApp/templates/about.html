Information Retrieval in RDF-Data Survey

This survey is a part of the master thesis of Martin Gjerde and Ebba Fingarsen. It aims to compare 3 different search methods on two datasets of structured RDF-data.

We will not keep any information about who is completing the survey. The goal of the survey is not to test you, and there are no right or wrong answers. The aim is to assess the users' percieved performance of different search methods.

What will you do?

You will be presented with a query and the information need/intent behind the query. Your task is to rate how relevant you perceive each result, given to be compared to the given query and intent.

Each result should be ranked with a score on a scale of 0-3 where 0 is not relevant and 3 is relevant. Each result has to be given a rating before you can submit the answer.
